# The Trust-First Opening Sequence (Days 1–21)

> The "earn it" stretch. Governed by `AUDIENCE.md` (who) + `VISION.md` (why). Every email here is a **give** —
> belonging, recognition, usefulness, a real gift — and the only asks are free, 2-minute, no-risk. No money is
> mentioned until trust is built (the existing founder-pricing arc picks up around Day 24, now *earned*).
>
> Rhythm: **3–4 gives for every ask.** Voice: the neighbor who started the town paper because they love the place.
> Tokens: `[First Name]` `[Business Name]` `[Community Name]` `[Category]` `[AM Name]`. CTA style: `[LABEL →]`.
