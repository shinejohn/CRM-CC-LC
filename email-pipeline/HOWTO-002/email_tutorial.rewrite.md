SUBJECT: We'd like to run your story
PREVIEW: The kind a neighbor reads, then walks in.

BODY:
Hi [First Name],

Every week, neighbors in [Community Name] read about a local business over their morning coffee — and a few of them think, *I should go there,* and then they do.

We'd like the next one to be [Business Name].

Not an ad. A real story — who you are, what you do, why folks keep coming back. The kind of thing that makes the town look at you a little differently, and that hangs on your wall for years.

You don't have to be a writer, and you can't get it wrong:

[TELL US YOUR STORY →]

We'll ask you a few simple questions, you answer in your own words, and we do the polishing. We don't reject submissions — if it needs work, we work on it with you until it sounds like you.

Here's why we bother: a town is better off when it knows its own — when people pick the [Category] down the street because they've heard the story behind it, not because an app pointed them there.

[AM Name]
[Community Name] Day.News

*P.S. — Stuck on where to start? Hit reply with the one thing about [Business Name] you wish more people in town knew. That's almost always the story.*
