# HOW AI "LEARNS" ABOUT BUSINESSES

This is crucial. If you understand this, you understand everything about the new discovery landscape.

AI assistants don't just search the web in real-time. They're trained on data. They're continuously crawling and indexing information. They build a "knowledge base" of what exists, what's reputable, what's relevant.

**For AI to recommend your business, AI has to know your business exists.**

How does AI learn about you?

**1. Authoritative Sources**
AI trusts certain sources more than others. News publications. Established directories. Verified platforms. When your business appears in trusted sources, AI weighs that heavily.

**2. Consistent Information**
AI looks for consistency. If your business name, address, services, and reputation appear consistently across multiple sources, AI is more confident about recommending you.

**3. Rich Context**
AI understands context. Articles about your business. Event listings. Community mentions. Reviews and testimonials. The richer the context, the better AI understands what you do and who you serve.

**4. Reputation Signals**
AI looks for signals of quality. Reviews. Ratings. Community engagement. Awards. Mentions. These signals help AI determine whether to recommend you—or your competitor.

**5. Freshness**
AI values current information. Businesses that are active—posting events, getting reviews, creating content—signal vitality. Stale listings suggest a business that may not even exist anymore.
